Common Questions

Everything you need to know

Straight answers to the questions we hear most often.

What's included in the free audit?

The free audit covers the fundamentals that make the biggest difference for most businesses. You'll receive an SEO health score with the most critical issues flagged, a page speed performance snapshot showing how quickly your site loads on mobile and desktop, and a mobile usability check to identify any experience problems for smartphone users.

We'll also outline your top 5 quick-win recommendations — the changes most likely to move the needle fastest — alongside a competitor snapshot showing how one of your main rivals is positioned in search.

How long does the audit take?

The free audit is typically delivered within 48 hours of your initial call — often sooner. We don't use automated tools and fire a PDF at you; every audit is reviewed and written by a human who understands your specific context.

The full audit takes up to 5 business days because it's a thorough piece of work — in-depth competitor analysis, keyword research, technical crawls, and a 30-page written report all take time to do properly.

Do I need to give you access to anything?

For the free audit, all we need is your website URL. Everything we assess at that level is publicly accessible — the same data Google uses to evaluate your site.

For the full audit, sharing read-only access to your Google Analytics and Google Search Console accounts gives us significantly richer insight into your real traffic patterns, which pages are underperforming, and which search terms you're almost ranking for. It's not required, but it makes the recommendations more precise and actionable.

What happens after the audit?

We'll walk you through every finding on a dedicated review call — in plain English, without the jargon. You'll come away with a clear understanding of what's holding your website back, and a prioritised list of actions ranked by their likely impact.

There's no obligation whatsoever. If you want to take the report and implement everything yourself, that's absolutely fine. If you'd like Foxhue to handle some or all of the work, we'll put together a proposal. No pressure, no hard sell.

Is this really free? What's the catch?

Yes, it's genuinely free. No credit card, no commitment, no strings attached. You won't be signed up to a mailing list or chased with daily calls.

We offer the free audit because we've found it's the most honest way to demonstrate what we do. If the report is useful and you want to work with us, that's great. If not, you still walk away with a useful document. We're confident enough in the quality of our work that we're happy to let it speak for itself.

Do you work with businesses outside Oxfordshire?

Absolutely. We're based in Bicester, Oxfordshire, but SEO and website work is entirely location-independent. We work with clients right across the UK — from London to Edinburgh — and our process works just as well over video call as it does in person.

If you're a local Oxfordshire or Buckinghamshire business, we're always happy to meet in person. If you're further afield, everything we do translates seamlessly online.

Still got a question we haven't answered? We're happy to help.
