Section 01

Executive Summary

  • The Bicester/Oxfordshire creative agency market has few dedicated design agencies — most competitors are marketing-first businesses offering web design as a secondary service.
  • There is a strong opportunity to own “design agency Bicester” and “branding agency Oxfordshire” keywords with minimal competition from incumbents.
  • Competitors focus heavily on SEO, PPC, and social media services — creative design, branding, and visual identity work is genuinely underserved in the local market.
  • Local SEO represents the single biggest opportunity: Google Business Profile, directory listings, and location-specific content pages will be key differentiators in a market where few agencies have optimised for this.
  • A content strategy built around design thinking, brand transformation case studies, and SME guides will fill a real gap that no current local competitor is addressing.

Section 05

Technical Recommendations

Competitors have adequate but not exceptional technical foundations. A well-optimised Foxhue site will outperform on technical SEO signals from launch.

Mobile-First, Fast Site

Build with a mobile-first approach. Competitors are adequate on mobile but none are exceptional. Speed is a ranking factor and a trust signal.

HTTPS Throughout

Full SSL coverage on all pages. Non-negotiable for trust, rankings, and Chrome browser warnings.

Schema Markup

Implement LocalBusiness, Organization, and Service schema types. Add Article schema for case studies and blog posts.

Core Web Vitals

Target passing scores on LCP, FID/INP, and CLS. Optimise images, defer non-critical scripts, minimise layout shifts.

Image Optimisation

Serve images in WebP/AVIF formats with lazy loading on below-fold content. Compress all portfolio images without visible quality loss.

Structured Data for Case Studies

Use Article schema with author, date, and image markup. Rich results in search will increase click-through rates.

Section 06

Content Strategy Recommendations

Publishing Cadence

Launch blog with a minimum of 2 articles per month. Consistency matters more than volume. Each article should target a specific keyword from the opportunity list above.

Recommended Topics

  • “How much does a brand refresh cost?”
  • “When does your business need rebranding?”
  • “DIY vs agency design — what to know”
  • “Brand identity checklist for startups”
  • “Web design trends for SMEs 2026”
  • “Why your logo isn’t a brand”

Pillar Pages

Create comprehensive, long-form pillar pages for each core service. These will serve as SEO anchors and internal linking hubs. Each pillar page should be 1,500–2,500 words with internal links to related blog posts and case studies.

Location Pages

Build dedicated location pages for surrounding towns: Bicester, Oxford, Banbury, and Buckingham. Each page should include localised content, client testimonials from that area (where available), and area-specific service descriptions.

Case Studies

Publish a minimum of 1 case study per quarter. Start with MARR Laser & Skin Clinic. Each case study should follow a consistent structure: challenge, approach, outcome, and measurable results.

Section 07

Local SEO Checklist

Local SEO is the single highest-impact, lowest-effort channel for Foxhue. Most competitors have not fully optimised for local search, creating a window of opportunity.

  • Set up Google Business Profile with full business details, service descriptions, and high-quality photos
  • List on Yell, Thomson Local, Yelp, Clutch, and DesignRush directories
  • Get listed in Oxfordshire-specific business directories and chambers of commerce
  • Encourage Google reviews from every client engagement (aim for 10+ reviews in first 6 months)
  • Add LocalBusiness schema markup to every page of the website
  • Create and publish Google Posts regularly (minimum weekly)
  • Ensure NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency across all directory listings and website pages
  • Upload geotagged images to Google Business Profile
  • Respond to all reviews (positive and negative) within 24 hours
  • Set up and optimise Apple Business Connect listing
