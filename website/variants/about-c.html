Our Story

The Foxhue Story

Foxhue started with a simple idea: small businesses deserve the same quality of design and marketing that big brands take for granted. We saw too many local businesses held back by DIY branding and cookie-cutter websites that didn't reflect who they actually were.

"Good design isn't a luxury. It's how people decide whether to trust you."

So we set up shop in Bicester, Oxfordshire, and got to work. Every project starts with a real conversation. We listen before we design. We ask hard questions. And we don't move forward until we understand what your business actually needs, not just what looks nice on a mood board.

We're proud to work with clients who care about doing things properly. Whether that's a complete brand identity, a website that converts, or a pitch deck that lands the deal, we bring the same level of attention and craft to every brief.
