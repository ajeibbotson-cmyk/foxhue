Common questions

Things people usually ask

How much does a typical project cost?

It depends on the scope, but most of our projects fall between £1,500 and £8,000. A logo design starts from around £800, while a full brand identity with website can range from £3,000 to £10,000+. We'll always give you a clear, fixed quote before any work begins.

How long does a project take?

Timelines vary by project. A logo design typically takes 3-4 weeks, a brand identity 4-6 weeks, and a website 6-10 weeks. We'll agree on a timeline together before we start and keep you updated at every stage.

Do you work with businesses outside Oxfordshire?

Absolutely. While we're based in Bicester, we work with clients across the UK. Most of our communication happens over video calls, email, and shared project boards, so location is rarely a barrier.

What do I need to provide to get started?

Just an idea of what you need and a willingness to have a conversation. We'll guide you through the rest. If you have existing brand assets, a rough brief, or examples of work you admire, that's helpful too -- but not essential.
