Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a typical project cost?

It depends on scope, but most of our projects fall between 2,000 and 10,000. We always provide a detailed quote before any work begins, so there are no surprises. A logo design starts from around 1,500, a full brand refresh from 3,000, and a website from 3,500.

How long does a project take?

Most projects take between 4 and 8 weeks from kickoff to delivery. A logo or brand refresh is typically 4 to 6 weeks. A website is 6 to 8 weeks. We will give you a clear timeline at the start and keep you updated throughout.

What is your design process?

We follow a four-stage process: Discover, Design, Deliver, and Evolve. We start by understanding your business, audience, and goals. Then we explore creative directions, refine the chosen approach, and deliver production-ready assets. After launch, we stay on to help your brand grow.

Do you work with clients outside Bicester?

Absolutely. While we are based in Bicester, Oxfordshire, we work with clients across the UK and beyond. Most of our communication happens over video calls, email, and shared project tools, so location is never a barrier.
