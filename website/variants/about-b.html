Who We Are

Small studio, big thinking

We are a creative design and marketing studio based in Bicester. Small by choice, so you always work directly with the people who do the work.

Our Story

Why We Started Foxhue

After years working inside agencies and in-house teams, we saw the same problem everywhere: small businesses getting overlooked. They had great products, loyal customers, and real ambition, but their brands did not reflect any of it.

So we built Foxhue to fix that. A studio that gives SMEs the same quality of creative thinking that big brands take for granted, but without the overheads, the layers, and the ego.

“We believe every business deserves a brand that matches its ambition. No exceptions, no excuses.”

With over 30 years of combined experience across brand, digital, and print, we bring depth and discipline to every project. We are not trying to be the biggest studio. We are trying to be the most useful one.

We work with businesses across Oxfordshire and beyond. Every project starts with listening and ends with results you can measure.

Our Values

01

Clarity

We cut through the noise. Every decision has a reason, every element earns its place, and we explain our thinking in plain English.

02

Craft

Details matter. We obsess over typography, spacing, colour, and consistency because the small things are what make brands feel professional.
