About Us

Creative people, real results

We are a small, focused creative studio. No bloated teams or layers of management — just experienced designers and marketers who genuinely care about your business.

Our Story

Built on experience, driven by craft

Foxhue was founded by experienced designers and marketers with a shared belief: that great design is not just about looking good — it is about solving problems and growing businesses.

With 30+ years of combined experience across branding, web design, digital marketing, and print, we have worked with businesses of all sizes, from local independents to national brands. We are based in Bicester, Oxfordshire, and we work with clients across the UK.

We keep things simple. You will work directly with the people doing the work — no account managers, no handoffs, no surprises. Just clear communication, honest advice, and design that delivers.
